About The Institute

Dr. Akhilesh Das Gupta Institute of Professional Studies, formerly known as Dr. Akhilesh Das Gupta Institute of Technology & Management ( ADGITM ), New Delhi was established by BBDES, LUCKNOW in the year 2003. ADGIPS has an aesthetically designed campus spread over approximately 8.08 acres of lush green environment at Shastri Park. The Campus comprises of five interconnected blocks covering a built up area of approx. 23,636.67 square meters. ADGITM offers Under Graduate and Post Graduate level full time Professional programs approved by AICTE in affiliation with Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University ( GGSIPU ), Delhi.

About The Conference

ICA2I 2024 is the premier International Conference on Advances in Artificial Intelligence, uniting leading researchers, scholars, and industry professionals to explore the latest breakthroughs, trends, and real-world applications in AI. Join us for insightful discussions, networking opportunities, and invaluable insights into the rapidly evolving landscape of artificial intelligence. Together, we'll delve into cutting-edge research and shape the future of AI technology across diverse sectors.
